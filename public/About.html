About

What is Uni Study?

Uni Studi is an online service designed to assist college students with their classes. Through a tutor/student system, people may join as tutors to help other students by using TokBox, a video chatting service.

How do I sign up?

You can sign up at our home page by clicking Teach or Learn depending on what you are looking to get out of our service.

Is it free?

Students get a free 30 day trial if they sign up with their school email account.

What's in it for tutors?

Tutors are generously payed by us based on a review system. After the tutoring session, the student can rate the tutor out of 5 and depending on the score, we allocate how much the tutor receives.
